Hi, I’m Natthan Davis, a graphic designer/marketer based out of Vancouver BC. I'm all about building, learning, and embracing challenges. Whether it's crafting projects or refining lifelong skills, I thrive on hands-on experiences. Before becoming a graphic designer, I worked with various companies as a marketing manager or analyst providing creative solutions when building advertising campaigns, business plans, or pitch decks.
