About

Hi, I’m Natthan Davis, a graphic designer/marketer based out of Vancouver BC. I'm all about building, learning, and embracing challenges. Whether it's crafting projects or refining lifelong skills, I thrive on hands-on experiences. Before becoming a graphic designer, I worked with various companies as a marketing manager or analyst providing creative solutions when building advertising campaigns, business plans, or pitch decks. Enjoying the fast paced environment of startups, I found myself working in a venture Capital fund, Tabula Rasa Ventures, ran by Yale MBA’s ultimately leading me to enrol back in school to further my own education.

Education

Having completed my marketing management diploma and on the last few credits of my BBA specializing in Business Intelligence at BCIT, I bring strong organizational and analytical skills to any design project. My time at BCIT gave me the time and space to refine my presentation and public speaking skills along with improving my managerial skillset.
